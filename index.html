Books

Gone Without Goodbyes (A Fated Love #1)

A Regency Romance

In the opulent yet rigidly stratified world of Regency England, "Gone Without Goodbyes" tells the poignant story of Alexander, the young heir to a dukedom, and Amelia, the rector's kind and resilient daughter. From a tender age, Amelia is infatuated with Alexander, often trailing after him. Though initially dismissive, Alexander's heart softens when Amelia helps him in his moments of anguish. Their love blossoms against a backdrop of societal expectations and familial obligations, challenging them to defy the conventions that seek to keep them apart. In a tale where happy endings are not guaranteed, "Gone Without Goodbyes" explores love, specially one that comes too late.

Bound By Betrayal (A Fated Love #2)

A Regency Romance

In the serene countryside of Regency England, Henry Fitzroy’s life appears perfect, marked by a successful estate and a seemingly blissful marriage to Lady Frances. However, when a tragic carriage accident unravels a web of deceit, Henry’s world is turned upside down. Lady Frances’ secret affair with Sir Benjamin Churchill shocks him to his core.

United by shared grief and betrayal, Henry and Sir Benjamin’s wife, Lady Georgiana Churchill, find solace in each other’s company. As they piece together the fragments of their shattered lives, an unexpected bond forms between them. Henry and Georgiana grapple with their growing feelings and the weight of their responsibilities. Their love, born from heartbreak, promises redemption but tests the limits of duty and honor.
